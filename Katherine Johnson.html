Katherine Johnson

About Katherine

Her work helped send astronauts to the Moon!

An American mathematician well-known for her aeronautics work at NASA, where she developed calculations that helped the US launch its first astronaut into space in 1961 and safely land Apollo 11 on the moon in 1969, among other pioneering crewed space missions

“I like to learn. That's an art and a science. Like what you do, and then you will do your best.”

- Katherine Johnson
